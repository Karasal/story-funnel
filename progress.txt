## Company
- **Name**: MONOMYTH MOTION PICTURES
- **Service**: Cinematic 5-minute personal documentaries
- **Location**: Calgary, Alberta
- **Pricing**: $500 (Documentary) / $1,500 (Full Package with landing page + 30-day AI marketing)
- **Payment**: 50/50 split — $250 upfront, $250 on delivery
- **Filming**: 2 days, 4 hours each, on location (5 days/20hrs for Full Package)
- **Turnaround**: 72-hour edit, unlimited revisions
- **Raw files**: Free transfer if client brings 2TB+ drive
- **Brand philosophy**: The Monomyth / Hero's Journey — every life follows the same narrative arc

## Design System
- **Aesthetic**: Dark cinematic neobrutalism — cinema camera meets indie film slate
- **Colors**: Near-black bg (#0D0D0D) with subtle blue radial gradient, white text, red accents (#FF0A0A)
- **Fonts**: Bebas Neue (display), Inter (body), JetBrains Mono (tech/labels)
- **Effects**: Animated film grain (grain.gif overlay), scanlines, scroll reveals
- **Neobrutalism**: Hard offset shadows (4-8px, zero blur), 3px borders, translate hover/press physics
- **Unique elements**: Clapperboard/slate UI, Hero's Journey circle diagram (animated SVG), film strip divider
